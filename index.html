Art 103 - YingHong Yuan

about YingHong Yuan...

Yinghong is a digital media artist. She was born in Guangzhou, Guangdong Province, China. Now she is studying for a Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in digital media art from San Jose State University, California. Yinghong's works are inspired by her reflection on some social problems and her expression of personal feelings. At this stage, most of her works are video art and installation art. Yinghong thinks about social problems and integrates her ideas into the works of art that are presented to the audience with visual effects. Her goal is to use digital media art to convey something that affects and helps society. In Yinghong's works, you can learn how the environment and some devices can produce unexpected results through correct fusion with images or videos.

San Jose State University, Digital Media Art Student
